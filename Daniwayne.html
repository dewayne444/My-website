✨ 20 Random Fun Facts ✨

🐙 Octopus Hearts

An octopus has three hearts, and two stop beating when it swims.

🍫 Chocolate Currency

The ancient Aztecs used cacao beans as currency.

🦒 Giraffe Sleep

Giraffes only sleep about 30 minutes a day.

🌋 Volcano Lightning

Volcanic eruptions can create lightning storms.

🐧 Penguin Proposals

Male penguins propose by giving females a pebble.

🎵 Music in Space

Astronaut Chris Hadfield sang “Space Oddity” on the ISS.

🦋 Butterfly Taste

Butterflies taste with their feet.

🍌 Banana Radiation

Bananas are naturally radioactive due to potassium.

🐝 Bee Dance

Bees communicate by dancing to show where food is.

🦑 Giant Squid Eyes

Giant squids have eyes the size of dinner plates.

🌍 Earth’s Speed

Earth spins at about 1,600 km/h at the equator.

🐨 Koala Prints

Koala fingerprints are so similar to humans they can confuse crime scenes.

🦖 Dinosaur Feathers

Many dinosaurs actually had feathers, not scales.

🍕 Pizza in Space

Pizza has been delivered to astronauts in orbit.

🐌 Snail Nap

Snails can sleep for up to three years.

🦈 Shark Age

Greenland sharks can live over 400 years.

🌕 Moon Smell

Astronauts said moon dust smells like gunpowder.

🐘 Elephant Memory

Elephants really do have excellent memories.

🦜 Parrot Vocabulary

Some parrots can learn over 1,000 words.

🌌 Universe Expansion

The universe is expanding faster than scientists expected.
